What our model predicts

Our regression model predicts the Fire Weather Index (FWI). FWI is a standardized index that quantifies fire danger by combining weather variables (temperature, relative humidity, wind speed, and precipitation). Higher FWI values indicate greater potential fire intensity and faster spread â€” which helps authorities prioritize resources.

  • Input: temperature, humidity, wind, rain (from dataset)
  • Output: predicted FWI (numeric â€” higher means more severe)
  • Use case: early-warning, allocation of firefighting resources, risk mapping
