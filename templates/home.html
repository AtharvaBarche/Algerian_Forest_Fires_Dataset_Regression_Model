Field Instructions

  • Temperature (°C): Daily average temperature in Celsius.
  • Relative Humidity (%): Daily mean relative humidity.
  • Wind Speed (km/h): Daily mean wind speed.
  • Rain (mm): Total rainfall for the day.
  • FFMC: Fine Fuel Moisture Code – moisture content of surface litter and fine fuels.
  • DMC: Duff Moisture Code – moisture content of loosely compacted organic layers.
  • ISI: Initial Spread Index – rate of fire spread depending on wind and FFMC.
  • Classes: Categorical outcome (fire or not fire) in the dataset.
  • Region: Dataset region (1 = Bejaia, 2 = Sidi Bel-abbes).
